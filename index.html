About Us

In 2003, Apple Inc. was a prominent tech company known for its innovative products. Led by CEO Steve Jobs, Apple had already made its mark with the Macintosh computer in the 1980s and the iconic iPod in the early 2000s. In 2003, it continued to focus on cutting-edge technology and design.The year saw the release of macOS Panther, a significant update to the Mac operating system, enhancing user experience and introducing features like Exposé and FileVault. The iTunes Music Store was also gaining traction, revolutionizing the music industry by offering legal digital music downloads.
